User Requirement Definition

• Allow customer to register into the system. • Allow customer to log into the system. • Choose a health center • The customer has the ability to request for an appointment. • The customer can give a feedback for the doctor. • Allow customer to logout of the system.

ABOUT US

Health centers is a mini hospital provides a basic health services such as internal medicine, family medicine and dentistry. Most of their patients are locals who caries government health insurance seeking for basic treatment. To get a treatment you just head for any health center and get an appointment then you have to wait. Waiting time can be several minutes or several hours, it depends on how many patients came before you.

SERVICES

• Let the patient Books an appointment from home. • no waiting time. • Providing an electronic interface to make interacting with the health center easier. • The patient can interact with the doctor after the appointment and providing feedback. • Reduce the patients numbers in the health center to help against corona virus.
